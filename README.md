# encInterface
ESP32 based encoder interface
#### Features
* works together with encDisplay
#### GPIO UART
* GPIO 16 - RX
* GPIO 17 - TX
#### GPIO Inputs
* GPIO 4 - In 1 1
* GPIO 18 - In 1 2
* GPIO 19 - In 1 3
* GPIO 21 - In 2 1
* GPIO 22 - In 2 2
* GPIO 23 - In 2 3
* GPIO 25 - In 3 1
* GPIO 26 - In 3 2
* GPIO 27 - In 3 3
* GPIO 32 - In 4 1
* GPIO 33 - In 4 2
* GPIO 34 - In 4 3
* GPIO 35 - In 5 1
* GPIO 36 - In 5 2
* GPIO 39 - In 5 3
#### GPIO Outputs
* GPIO 5 - Out 1 1
* GPIO 13 - Out 1 2
* GPIO 14 - Out 1 3
